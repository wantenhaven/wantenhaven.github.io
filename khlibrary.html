Knowhaven Library

The dust sticks to your feet as you reach the area where the wall meets the floor, that area itself hidden by large walls of dust-bunnies. Surprisingly, walking through the walls was easy, the dust walls were soft, powdery and barely solid. Past the wall you feast your eyes on a small curated library. Small shelves lined with even smaller books. Good thing you are equally as small.

The format of these books are based off of the concept of digital books, a format Wanten had found out through Butterick's Practical Typography website.
