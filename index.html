Big font and library update!

April 15, 2025

Did a huge overhaul of the fonts!

I chose google fonts for its very open lisences and its ease of browsing. The fonts used in this website(as the time of writing this) are:

Domine by Impallari Type for the main text

Winky Rough by Typofactur for the title

Patua One by LatinoType for the headers

Out of all of these, I am the most on the fence about Patua One. It seems to be a small size font based off of the description and seems a tad too loud on bigger headers.

For how I finally came to the decision on which fonts to choose, I consulted with the book The Graphic Design School's chapter on fonts. From that chapter, my biggest takeaway was the importance of the history of each font has on its use-case and mood. And another is when in doubt, trust the typographer's description!
